# Kokoro – Your Energy Balance Sheet

## What is Kokoro?

Kokoro is your personal **energy management companion**. Just like a financial balance sheet tracks money in and out, Kokoro helps you understand and balance your physical, mental, and emotional energy throughout the day.

We all know that feeling-some days you're running on empty, other days you're unstoppable. Kokoro helps you see _why_. By tracking simple energy snapshots and reflecting on what affects your well-being, you gain clarity about your patterns and learn to make better choices for yourself.

It's not another productivity app telling you what to do. It's a tool that _listens_ to you and helps you understand yourself better.

---

## Why Kokoro?

- **Understand Your Energy** – See patterns in how different activities, people, and choices impact your well-being
- **Simple & Intuitive** – Takes seconds to log, not another complicated habit tracker
- **Always With You** – Works seamlessly on your phone, tablet, and devices
- **Your Private Journal** – Your energy data stays yours. No algorithms, no judgment
- **Actionable Insights** – Discover what truly drains and energizes you
